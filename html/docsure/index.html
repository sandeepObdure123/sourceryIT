Hi! This is DOCSURE

A decentralized Blockchain based certificate issuance application designed by Us
Provide issuance and verification of official records.

Issuance of verifiable, renewable, revokable
academic credentials, professional certifications,
workforce development, and civic records.

Issuance

Issuance of certificate created with a predefined template, stored on IPFS with updated metadata, with lifetime/timed expiry and hash of metadata alongwith the filehash and other metadata on permissioned Hyperledger fabric network for verification at any stage of time.

Sharing

The certificate soft copy issued contain link to application to compare the data on certificate with the data stored on Blockchain, to add an extra layer of trust on the validation of data.

Renew/Expire

Certificate issued can be renewed with mapping to the previously issued certificate, with signature from the authorised entity .
